Mapexplorer

View Project

An interactive map for the game thetravelers.online made with javascript canvas.

https://pfg.pw/mapexplorer

Controls: Move and pan with the mouse or touch, Zoom with scroll or pinch, Select tile with click or tap.

Hotkeys:

  • G: go to coords
  • K: render huge (when zoomed out)
  • M: toggle 'm' visualization
